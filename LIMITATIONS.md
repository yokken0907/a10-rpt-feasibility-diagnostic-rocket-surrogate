# Limitations

## Surrogate limitations

- The surrogate is low-dimensional and nondimensional.
- It is not calibrated to a specific engine, propellant, injector, chamber, material system, cooling channel, sensor suite, or flight controller.
- Resource scales are abstract authority multipliers, not hardware mass, turbopump size, propellant-flow multipliers, or cooling-channel geometry.
- The stress profiles are nondimensional tests, not real flight environments.

## Control limitations

- The work does not prove a formal barrier certificate.
- Results depend on the selected stress profiles, controller parameterization, resource scales, and evaluation horizon.
- Harsh combined stress remains unresolved under the tested family.
- Higher-fidelity thermal, acoustic, fluid, structural, and actuator models are required before any engineering interpretation.

## Repository limitations

- Large raw per-seed sweep CSVs are not included in the GitHub body.
- Virtual environments and compiled dependencies are excluded.
- This archive is a paper companion, not a certified one-command validation package.
